About the Event

DUX Day is an all day mini-conference. The theme is mastering prototyping tools, and together we will do just that. Learn from experts in the community as they show you what you can do with a plethora of prototyping tools. Speakers will be highlighting InVision, Axure and Framer.

The day will start with a speaker relating to our natural inclination to avoid prototyping and why its so important that we do. We will get a great introduction or refresher into the foundation of paper prototyping. And then speakers will cover each of our three tools, sharing the pros and the cons and why you might want to use it. We will round out the day by breaking into workshops. Attendees choose which tool they want to focus on and join together to create prototypes using it.
