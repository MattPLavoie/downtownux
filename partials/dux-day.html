About the Event

DUX Day is an all day mini-conference. The theme is mastering prototyping tools, and together we will do just that. Learn from experts in the community as they show you what you can do with a plethora of prototyping tools. Speakers will be highlighting Invision, Axure and Framer.

Bring your laptop loaded with these tools, because the afternoon will include workshops. Decide which tool you want to dive in with, and get hands on experience in a small class format creating prototypes with the pros.

Sponsored By

This event is sponsored by TEKsystems who are generously providing morning coffee and lunch for all attendees. They are incredible partners for our community and will be available to chat if you'd like to connect with them.

Sponsoring

We are also very happy to be giving back as a community by sponsoring Codecraft Lab. Codecraft Lab is a 501(c)3 non-profit organization that is making a difference in Brevard County by teaching kids how to code. One-hundred percent of the ticket proceeds are going directly to their organization. Thank you for helping us make a difference as a community!
