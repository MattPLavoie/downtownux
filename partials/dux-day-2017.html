Navigating Your Own Path

Gus Hernandez & Alejandro Campos

So you’re tired of working for “the man” and want to strike out on your own? Great! But as you have probably noticed, there’s a lot more to it than just posting some work on Dribbble and updating your LinkedIn profile.

Join presenters Gus Hernandez and Alejandro Campos as they cover the basics of starting your own gig, client and project management and the importance of establishing an ethical compass that guides your business philosophy and work ethic. The workshop will end with a portfolio review so if you’ve been seeking feedback, this will be a great opportunity to get feedback from industry professionals who have been working with interactive design for 20+ years.

Data-Driven UX Design

Heather O’Neill

The face of UX is changing, and it’s no longer enough to rely on instinct and personal experience to create relevant designs. To move to the next level, your designs need to be based on data and relevant to the overall business structure. This might seem obvious in theory, but how do you make it happen?

Join UX strategist and business consultant Heather O’Neill, CEO of Pixels for Humans, for a hands-on, in-depth exploration of creating and improving designs using data. In this workshop, you’ll learn how to include data as a core component of your design process, and how to design your own experiments for collecting the right data every time. You’ll learn how to determine what data to collect, how to devise and run experiments to collect the data you need, and how to use data to make better design decisions.

Designing for Humans: Introduction to Design Thinking

Jenae Ramos

In designing for humans, we must embrace being human.

Led by Jenae Ramos, this workshop guides you through the journey of human-centered design. Choose a journey from predefined scenarios to solve real-world problems using the design thinking methodology. Engage in group learning to have fun and apply design thinking skills. Learn more about the process from empathy and user research to experimentation and prototyping.

Whether you’re a designer, a developer, product owner, or design enthusiast, find out how you can apply design thinking to your projects and business goals.

Jenae is a UX architect at NCR Corporation, who contributes to R&D and international consulting projects. She works across many lines of business including health, hospitality, retail, and financial.

Usability Testing From Strategy to Practice

Matt Lavoie

Every piece of software with an interface has one thing in common, people. And where people are involved there will always be hidden gaps in usability we are blind to. One of the most effective ways to uncover these issues is through usability testing.

Anyone can facilitate a usability test, but there are some important techniques you must learn to ensure you receive unbiased results. In this hands on training we will practice planning and conducting usability tests. Beyond practically facilitating usability testing, we will also consider how to make our research effective, engage our teams, and execute on our findings.

This training will focus on applied practice, and we will learn these things by doing them. Attendees are encouraged to bring a product they would like to test to the workshop.
