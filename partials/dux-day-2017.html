About the Event

DUX Day is an all day mini-conference. Last year's theme was mastering prototyping tools. This year we will be diving deeper into the practices that can help us master our craft. Learn from experts in the community as they dive into the practices that distinguish their work and help them excel in their UX practice.

Sponsored By

This event is sponsored by TEKsystems Digital and Creative Services who are generously providing morning coffee and lunch for all attendees. They are incredible partners for our community and will be available to chat if you'd like to connect with them.

Sponsoring

We are also very happy to be giving back as a community for the second year in a row by sponsoring Codecraft Lab. Codecraft Lab is a 501(c)3 non-profit organization that is making a difference in Brevard County by teaching kids how to code. One-hundred percent of the ticket proceeds are going directly to their organization. Thank you for helping us make a difference as a community!

Downtown UX

So what is the Downtown UX Meetup you ask? We are a monthly UX meetup in the Downtown Orlando area. Even if you've never joined us before, you are more than welcome to join us for DUX Day. To learn more about our past events, check us out on Meetup.com.
